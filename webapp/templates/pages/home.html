Frequently Asked Questions

How to apply as Old Student?

First you need to get a CoG (Certificate of Grades) at the registrar, after that the University will announce where/when the Enrollment will happen. Then follow these steps:

Step 1: Society Fee. It should be announced at your Department.

Step 2: Fill-out Curriculum Checklist. Curriculum checklists are available at your society. Must be placed in a long brown folder, properly labeled with your name, course, and student number.

Step 3: Evaluation of grades & Advising of Subjects to enroll. Wait for an announcement at your department where it will be held.

Step 4: Issuance of Queuing Number (per program).

Step 5: Encoding of Subjects. Your department will announce where it will be held.

How to apply as New Student?

If you are a New student, you should follow these easy steps:

Step 1: Admission Form. Fill-out and download the application form.

Step 2: Printing of Admission Form. Download and print the completed online application form and attach a 1x1 photo in the printed application with your affixed signature. Place it in a short white folder with all the required documents.

Step 3: Submission. Bring 2 white short folders and 2 photocopies of all your original documents. Submit them to the Guidance office of CvSU-Bacoor City.

Step 4: Examination Date. You will be given a permit and a schedule for the examination.

How to apply as Transferee?

To apply as a Transferee, you need the following:

Step 1: Accomplish the Application Form for Admission.

Step 2: Photocopy of Transcript of Records/Certificate of Grades.

Other requirements after Evaluation:

* Honorable Dismissal.

* Certificate of Good Moral Character.

* NBI or Police Clearance.

What is CVSU Mission?

Cavite State University shall provide excellent, equitable, and relevant educational opportunities in the arts, sciences, and technology through quality instruction and responsive research and development activities. It shall produce professional, skilled, and morally upright individuals for global competitiveness.

What is CVSU Bacoor Quality Policy?

We commit to the highest standards of education, value our stakeholders, strive for continual improvement of our products and services, and uphold the University’s tenets of Truth, Excellence, and Service to produce globally competitive and morally upright individuals.

How can I apply for scholarships at CvSU Bacoor?

To apply for scholarships at CvSU Bacoor, you need to inquire at the Scholarship Office of the university. They usually have specific requirements and application procedures for different scholarship programs.

We have already passed CTC until the 1st semester, our card is only valid, will we still pass an updated CTC?

If it's during admission, it's possible, but when we come out with the list for NOA, the card must be updated (1st to 4th quarter) and must have good moral indicated. The updated Card and Good Moral Certified True Copy (CTC) will be attached.

My documents are not Certified True Copy (CTC), can I forward them?

Upon admission, the requirements may not be CTC initially, but when we issue a list for NOA, the CTC requirements must specifically be passed, which is quite significant.

What does NOA mean? and What is NOA for?

NOA stands for "Notice of Admission". It is given to those who are eligible to enroll, informing them that they have been accepted to the university.

When is the deadline for submission of requirements?

The deadline depends on each course or quota. We will announce when we are nearing the admission period for each program.

Can I take a course that is not aligned with STRAND?

The course you take must align with your strand; it is an essential requirement.

Can the requirements be incomplete? Can I just follow?

The requirements must be complete for us to evaluate if you're qualified for the course. Incomplete requirements will hinder the process.
