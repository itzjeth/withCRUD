Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to the university?

First, you need to fill out online admission form, print your admission form, then you can submit your complete requirement in a folder to the Guidance Office for Evaluation, from that you will receive an email for Examination Date.

What are requirements for first year applicants (Grade 12 Students)

You need a photocopy of Grade 11 card, a Certification from the principal or Adviser indicationg that the applicant is currently enrolled as Grade 12 student with a strand indicated

Does CvSU have entrance exam 2024?

The CvSU Bacoor will the conduct of admission exam will be waived for 1st Semester, AY 2023-2024.

Is CvSU Bacoor still open for admission?

The application admission for first semester S.Y. 2024 - 2025 already begins on November 6, 2023

What is the passing grade in CvSU?

3.00 is the passing grade while 5.00 is the failing grade

What is a 2.00 grade equivalent to CvSU?

The grade requirement for transferee is GPA of 2.00 (equivalent of 85%) or better and no failed grade.
